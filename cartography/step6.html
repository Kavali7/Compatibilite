Understanding your map

Each coloured line on your map represents a different area of life. These lines show where a particular planet’s energy is amplified on Earth.

The Venus line

When you travel along your Venus line you may find love, harmony and deep emotional connection. It’s ideal for relationships and creativity.

The Moon line

Your Moon line points to places where you’ll feel safe, supported and at home. It enhances intuition and emotional wellbeing.

The Jupiter line

Jupiter lines are all about growth and expansion. They bring opportunity, success and confidence, supporting travel and personal growth.
